Solution: ████████
Answer: REDACTED

Written by Olga Vinogradova

This is a word search with some letters replaced by some black boxes (█). Each █ is intersected with an appropriately thematic word synonymous with "redacted" or equivalent. Reading the black box letters top to bottom and left to right gives the cluephrase COMPRESS VERTICAL. A table with all words is shown here.

The cluephrase is telling us we need to squish the grid vertically. To do that, we must first remove (or... redact) all the found words that got us the cluephrase. This gives a new grid shaped 12x12.

In the new word search there are two sets of words we have to find. In one set each of the 8 words is synonymous with "answer" or equivalent. The second set consists of 8 words five letters long and beginning with A-H. There is exactly one intersection per word between the two sets.

Author’s Notes

This answer slot was always intended to be rather troll-y and I was drawn to it like a cat to food. We hoped it could be rather approachable as a twist on something ordinary like a word search, but during hunt discovered that was not the case. Despite that we really enjoyed seeing teams label their puzzle sheets with the answer itself and hope it brought much groaning in the end. A silly suggestion was to make the extraction ANSWER IS THE PUZZLE TITLE but to my sadness I was outvoted. Maybe it was for the best, but I still dream it would have worked.
