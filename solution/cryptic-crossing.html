Next, we turn to the envelopes, whose addresses are sadly missing their street names. However, we can find several such names in the grid—some of our crossword answers form two-word phrases with the expanded versions of the street suffixes. (If we're not sure what some of the suffixes are, the U.S. Postal Service has a convenient list.)

Associating the crossword answers with their proper street extension and indexing using the address numbers yields the words NINETEEN D THIRTEEN A TWENTY D ONE D. Taking these four crossword answers (19, 20, and 1 Down, and 13 Across), we realize that these are yet more "streets", which we can place in the four blank postcards below.

Finally, taking the first letters of each suffix (as indicated by the red blanks) yields SEND, the answer to the puzzle.

Author’s Notes

This mechanic was originally inspired by a (failed) attempt to write a puzzle based on My Immortal. Unfortunately, (or perhaps fortunately, for the rest of you) EBONY DARK'NESS DEMENTIA RAVEN is a little too difficult to fit into a single line of cryptic crossword fill.
